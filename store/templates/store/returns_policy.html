Returns & Refunds

Our commitment to customer satisfaction and fair refund policies

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our content. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. This ensures you can try our products risk-free and get a full refund if they don't meet your expectations.

Refund Eligibility

Eligible for Refund

  • Technical issues preventing proper download or usage
  • Quality issues not apparent in the preview
  • Incorrect or misrepresented product descriptions
  • Duplicate purchases (accidental)

Not Eligible for Refund

  • Change of mind after downloading content
  • Exclusive rights purchases
  • Claims without valid reasons or documentation
  • Requests made after 30 days of purchase
