Producer FAQ

Everything you need to know about selling your sounds on WavHaven

To start selling on WavHaven:

  1. Create your WavHaven account
  2. Complete your producer profile
  3. Upload your first sound
  4. Set your pricing and licensing terms
  5. Start promoting your content

File requirements:

  • Beats/Loops: WAV, MP3, or AIFF format
  • Minimum quality: 44.1kHz, 16-bit
  • Maximum file size: 100MB for beats, 50MB for loops
  • Soundkits: ZIP files up to 500MB
  • All content must be original or properly licensed

Revenue sharing and pricing information:

  • You keep 80% of each sale
  • Payments processed securely through Stripe
  • Set your own prices within platform guidelines
  • Monthly payouts for balances over $50
  • Transparent sales analytics and reporting

Standard licensing options:

  • Basic License: Personal/Non-Profit Use
  • Premium License: Commercial Use
  • Exclusive Rights: Full ownership transfer
  • Custom licensing available for special cases

Tips for growing your presence:

  • Optimize your profile and sound descriptions
  • Use relevant tags for better discoverability
  • Share your WavHaven profile on social media
  • Engage with the community and collaborate
  • Maintain consistent upload schedule
